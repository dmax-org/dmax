# D-MAX Code of Conduct

We expect all participants in the D-MAX community to be respectful and constructive. Harassment or abusive behavior will not be tolerated.

## Our Standards

- Treat everyone with respect and empathy.  
- Be inclusive and considerate of diverse backgrounds and experiences.  
- Give constructive feedback, and be open to receiving feedback.  
- Avoid offensive language, personal attacks, or any form of harassment.  

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness a violation of this code of conduct:

- Open an issue in the repository marked as `code-of-conduct` OR  
- Contact the maintainers at `maintainer@dmax.org`  

All reports will be handled confidentially.
